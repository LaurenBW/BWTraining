The RISE Framework

RISE is BruntWork's framework for Client Success — your compass for moving from reactive task-completion to proactive, strategic partnership with every client you manage. Everything you learn in this training maps back to one of these four pillars. Click each one to explore it.

Core mindset
Value-first engagement: In every interaction, the question is not "what do I need from this client?" It's "what does this client need from me?" That shift in perspective is what RISE is built on.

Delivery builds trust

Strong relationships give clients the trust to share what's really going on. Honest information powers better strategy. Better strategy makes your execution more targeted and impactful. And consistent execution deepens the trust your clients have in you — which strengthens the relationship further. Miss any pillar and the whole cycle weakens. Build all four, and you stop completing tasks and start genuinely growing your clients' businesses alongside BruntWork.

You can also apply RISE to individual activities — a QBR, a check-in call, even a single email. Ask: who are my relationships here? What insights do I have? What's my strategy for this interaction? How will I execute and measure it?

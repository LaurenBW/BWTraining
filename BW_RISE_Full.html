The RISE Framework

What is RISE?

RISE is BruntWork's framework for Client Success. It stands for Relationships, Insights, Strategy and Execution — four pillars that together describe what it means to be an outstanding CSM.

The framework exists because great CSMs don't just complete tasks — they build trust, read signals, plan ahead, and deliver consistently. RISE gives you a common language for all of that. Everything you learn this week maps back to one of these four pillars.

The pillars are sequential and interdependent — each one enables the next. Strong relationships give you honest information. Honest information powers better strategy. Better strategy makes your execution more impactful. And consistent execution deepens the relationship further. Miss any one pillar and the whole cycle weakens.

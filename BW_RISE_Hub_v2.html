The RISE Framework

RISE is BruntWork's framework for Client Success — your compass for moving from reactive task-completion to proactive, strategic partnership with every client. Everything you learn maps back to one of these four pillars. Click each one to explore it fully.

Core mindset
Value-first engagement: Before every interaction, the question is not "what do I need from this client?" It's "what does this client need from me?" That shift in perspective is what the entire RISE framework is built on — and it's the hardest habit to build and the easiest to lose.

Strong relationships give clients the trust to share what's really happening. Honest information powers better strategy. Better strategy makes execution more impactful. And consistent execution deepens trust — raising the whole cycle to a higher level with every pass. You can also apply RISE to any individual activity: a QBR, a check-in call, even a single email. Ask: who are my relationships here? What insights do I have? What's my strategy? How will I execute and measure it?
